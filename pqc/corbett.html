Projects

Ramnagar, Uttarakhand (2020)

Interpretation Centre at
Corbett Tiger Reserve

Corbett Tiger Reserve’s biodiversity spans wetlands, grasslands, and woodlands, supporting diverse wildlife. Bird dioramas showcase avian species, while the nocturnal room reveals the reserve’s night life. The monsoon mechatronic exhibit highlights seasonal changes. Visitors can also explore the life of Jim Corbett, the legendary conservationist who inspired the reserve’s creation.

Brief

How might we design an engaging and immersive interpretation centre at Corbett Tiger Reserve that highlights its diverse ecosystems, showcases iconic wildlife such as the Bengal Tiger and Asiatic Elephant, and fosters conservation awareness, education, and appreciation for the legacy of Jim Corbett and the rich biodiversity of the reserve?

The Corbett Tiger Reserve Interpretation Centre showcases the diverse ecosystems of the region, from dense woodlands to thriving wetlands. It features immersive exhibits on the majestic Bengal Tiger, Asiatic Elephant, and rich birdlife. Through interactive displays and engaging storytelling, visitors gain a deeper appreciation of Corbett’s unique wildlife, seasonal transformations, and the enduring legacy of Jim Corbett in conservation efforts.
