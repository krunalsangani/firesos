Sahibganj, Jharkhand (2023)

Rajmahal Fossil Museum &
Interpretation Centre

Sahibganj in Jharkhand, part of the Rajmahal region, is known for its ancient tree fossils. A dedicated center showcases these fossils, explaining their formation through the fossilization process over millions of years. The site highlights the geological significance of Rajmahal’s fossil-rich landscape and its historical natural environment.

Brief

How might we create an engaging and immersive fossil interpretation center that effectively highlights the geological significance of Sahibganj, educates visitors on the fossilization process, and fosters curiosity, conservation, and appreciation for the region’s prehistoric landscape?

The Rajmahal Fossil exhibit unveils the prehistoric wonders of Sahibganj, Jharkhand, showcasing ancient tree fossils that date back millions of years. It highlights the fossilization process, preserving the region’s geological history, and offers a glimpse into the ancient landscapes that once thrived in the Rajmahal Hills.
