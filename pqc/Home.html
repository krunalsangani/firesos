We are Pugmark Qmulus Consortium

Revolutionize and understand the natural world

Pugmark Qmulus Consortium is a premier firm specializing in nature interpretation, exhibit design and planning, model making and fabrication, and digital content development. Since 2003, we have been dedicated to creating memorable experiences that connect people with nature.

We offer a wide range of nature interpretation products and services nationwide, with a particular focus on developing theme-based nature interpretation centers. Our team of expert interpreters and designers has extensive experience working in protected areas and zoos, ensuring that each project is informed by deep knowledge and passion.

Our integrated design approach helps clients express their identity and vision, resulting in exhibits that go beyond mere displays. We create captivating, immersive experiences that engage and inspire audiences. By incorporating interactive media, we ensure our designs are not only visually compelling but also leave a lasting impact.

PQC builds immersive Interpretation Centers that blend technology, storytelling, and interactive design to educate and inspire visitors.
