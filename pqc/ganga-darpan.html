Projects

Sarnath, Uttar Pradesh (2018)

Ganga Darpan
Interpretation Centre

The Ganga Darpan Interpretation Centre showcases the journey of the Ganges from Gangotri to Ganga Sagar, highlighting its connecting rivers and estuaries. Interactive displays focus on the issue of pollution in the Ganga and emphasize efforts like wastewater treatment and clean-up initiatives to restore the river’s health.

Brief

How might we design an engaging and immersive interpretation centre for the Ganga Darpan that highlights the sacred journey of the Ganges from Gangotri to Ganga Sagar, showcases its rich network of connecting rivers and estuaries, and fosters public awareness, education, and appreciation for the ecological significance of the river while addressing pollution, conservation efforts, and sustainable water management practices?

The Ganga Darpan Interpretation Centre offers an immersive and educational experience, tracing the sacred journey of the Ganges from Gangotri to Ganga Sagar. Through interactive exhibits, visitors can explore the river’s rich ecosystems, its cultural and ecological significance, and the ongoing conservation efforts to restore its health.
